\section{Conditional Distribution}
Let 
\begin{align}
\begin{bmatrix}
	X \\ Y
\end{bmatrix}
\sim \Norm \Bigg(
\begin{bmatrix}
	\mu_X \\ \mu_Y
\end{bmatrix},
\begin{bmatrix}
	\Sigma_{XX} & 	\Sigma_{XY} \\
	\Sigma_{YX} & 	\Sigma_{YY} \\
\end{bmatrix}
\Bigg)
\end{align}

For simplicity let's introduce a new letter for inverse covariance matrix:
\begin{align}
	\mathcal{S} = \begin{bmatrix}
		\Sigma_{XX} & 	\Sigma_{XY} \\
		\Sigma_{YX} & 	\Sigma_{YY} \\
	\end{bmatrix} ^ {-1} 
= \begin{bmatrix}
	\mathcal{S}_{XX} & 	\mathcal{S}_{XY} \\
	\mathcal{S}_{YX} & 	\mathcal{S}_{YY} \\
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align}
